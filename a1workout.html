Grammar Focus 💪

Simple Present for Routines

Use this for habits and actions you do regularly.

I / You / We / They + run
He / She / It + runs

Ability with 'Can' / 'Can't'

Use this to talk about what you are able to do. The verb form does not change.

(+) I can lift 50 KG.

(-) He can't run 10 kilometers.

Workout Numbers & Stats 📊

Q: How often do you run?
  • ➡️ I run three times a week.
  • ➡️ I go to the gym every day.
  • ➡️ I never exercise.
Q: How much can you bench press?
  • ➡️ I can bench 90 KG for 3 sets of 10 reps.
  • ➡️ I can lift 20 KG.
  • ➡️ I'm not sure. I don't lift weights.
