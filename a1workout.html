Simple Present for Routines

Use this for habits and actions you do regularly.

I / You / We / They + run
He / She / It + runs

Ability with 'Can' / 'Can't'

Use this to talk about what you are able to do. The verb form does not change.

(+) I can lift 50 KG.

(-) He can't run 10 kilometers.
