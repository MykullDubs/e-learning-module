Watching TV is a popular hobby. How can you talk about the shows you enjoy? This lesson will teach you the basic words and grammar to share your favorites.

Grammar Focus 📺

Expressing Preferences with 'Like'

You can use like + to watch or like + watching. At this level, they mean the same thing!

like + to watch

I like to watch comedies.

He likes to watch dramas.

like + watching

I like watching comedies.

He likes watching dramas.
