What Are You Reading?

CEFR Level: A2

Use the present progressive and simple past to talk about your reading habits.

Let's Discuss!

Are you reading a good book right now? In this lesson, we'll learn how to talk about the books you are reading these days and the books you finished in the past.

Present Progressive (be + verb-ing)

Use this for actions happening **now** or **around now** (these days, this week).

I + am reading.

He / She / It + is reading.

We / You / They + are reading.
