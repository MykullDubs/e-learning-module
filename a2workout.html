Adverbs of Frequency

These tell us **how often** something happens. They usually go **before** the main verb.

  • always (100%) I **always** stretch before a workout.
  • usually (90%) She **usually** goes to the gym after work.
  • often (70%) We **often** run in the park together.
  • sometimes (50%) I **sometimes** lift weights.
  • rarely (10%) He **rarely** skips a workout.
  • never (0%) They **never** exercise on Sundays.

Adverbs of Intensity

These tell us **how much** or to what degree. They modify adjectives or other adverbs.

  • The workout was really difficult.
  • I am very tired now.
  • This weight is quite heavy.
  • It was a little easy.
