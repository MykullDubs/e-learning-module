My Reading Habits

A1 Reading

Let's Begin!

Do you like to read? Reading is a great habit. In this lesson, we will learn the words to talk about when and what you read.

New Words

book

  • I want to read a new book.
  • The book you told me about is good.
  • He bought a book yesterday. (Past Tense)

magazine

  • She buys a magazine every week.
  • I read that magazine last night. (Past Tense)

library

  • There is a library near my house.
  • The library is a very quiet place.
  • We went to the library on Saturday. (Past Tense)
