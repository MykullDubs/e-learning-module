Grammar Focus 💡

Prepositions of Time: IN, AT, ON

Preposition When to Use Example
IN Parts of the day (the morning, the afternoon, the evening) I read in the morning.
AT Specific times, night He reads at night.
ON Days of the week, the weekend She reads on the weekend.

Simple Present Verbs: The '-s' Rule

I / You / We / They + read

"We read every day."

He / She / It + reads

"She reads at night."

Let's Talk About You 🗣️

Q: What do you like to read?

(What kind of books or magazines?)

  • 🚀 I like to read science fiction novels.
  • 🐉 I like to read fantasy books.
  • 📜 I like to read history books.
  • 💥 I like to read comic books.
Q: Where do you read?
  • 🏠 I read at home.
  • 🌳 I read in the park.
  • 🚌 I read on the bus.
Q: When do you read?
  • ☀️ I read in the morning.
  • 🌙 I read at night.
  • 📅 I read on the weekend.
