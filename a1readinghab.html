Prepositions of Time: IN, AT, ON

Preposition When to Use Example
IN Parts of the day I read in the morning.
AT Specific times, night He reads at night.
ON Days of the week She reads on the weekend.

Simple Present Verbs: The '-s' Rule

I / You / We / They + read

"We read every day."

He / She / It + reads

"She reads at night."
