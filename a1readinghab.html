My Reading Habits

CEFR Level: A1

Learn to talk about your routine with simple present verbs and prepositions of time.

Let's Begin!

Do you like to read? Reading is a great habit. In this lesson, we will learn the words to talk about when and what you read.

New Words

book

I read a book.

magazine

He reads a magazine.

quiet place

She reads in a quiet place.

Prepositions of Time: IN, AT, ON

Preposition When to Use Example
IN Parts of the day (the morning, the afternoon, the evening) I read in the morning.
AT Specific times, night He reads at night.
ON Days of the week, the weekend She reads on the weekend.

Simple Present Verbs

Remember to add '-s' to the verb for he, she, and it. (I read -> He reads)
