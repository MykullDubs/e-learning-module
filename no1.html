Understanding Contrast Connectors

Contrast connectors are essential for building a fair and complex review. They allow you to show a good point and a bad point together.

Connector Meaning Grammar Rule
but Shows a simple opposite or weakness. Used to join two clauses. (Comma before but.)
even though Shows a surprising opposite. (It's surprising that X is true because Y is also true.) Used to start a dependent clause. (Usually comma after the clause if it starts the sentence.)
however Introduces a strong contrasting idea (formal). Use between two full sentences. (Semicolon or period before however, comma after however.)

Example: The watch is beautiful, but it is expensive. OR: The watch is beautiful. However, it is expensive.
