Grammar Focus 🎯

Modals: Advice & Obligation

Use modals to give recommendations and state rules.

  • Should: (Advice) You should warm up before lifting weights.
  • Must: (Strong Obligation/Rule) You must wear proper shoes in the gym.
  • Have to: (External Obligation) I have to go to the gym today; it's on my schedule.

Gerunds & Infinitives

Some verbs are followed by a gerund (-ing), others by an infinitive (to + verb).

Verb + Gerund (-ing)

I enjoy running.

You should avoid lifting with bad form.

Verb + Infinitive (to...)

I want to get stronger.

He plans to hire a trainer.
