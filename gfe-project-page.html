Golden Fellows English

A full-scale Learning Management System (LMS) designed to deliver a premium, personalized learning experience for professional ESL students.

Role: Lead LXD & Full-Stack Developer
Timeline: 12 Weeks

The Challenge

The goal was to create a high-end ESL platform that could compete with major players by offering what they often lack: truly personalized feedback and direct access to instructors. This required building a complete ecosystem from the ground up, including a content library, user authentication, subscription management, and the infrastructure for instructor-led services.

My Approach & Process

I adopted the SAM (Successive Approximation Model) for this project, leveraging AI as a development partner to rapidly prototype and iterate. This allowed for a continuous "design, build, review" cycle, ensuring the final product was both technically robust and pedagogically sound.

  • Phase 1 (Savvy Start): Brainstormed and wireframed the core user flow for both students and instructors. Used AI to generate initial layouts for the dashboard and lesson pages.
  • Phase 2 (Iterative Development): Built out the frontend and backend in parallel, focusing on one feature at a time (e.g., authentication, then lesson library, then payments). This allowed for constant testing and refinement.
  • Phase 3 (Deployment & Feedback): Deployed the application on Vercel and integrated user feedback to make final improvements to the UI and functionality.
